Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

JackLinus AI ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website jacklinus.com, use our AI chat service, sign in with Google, or interact with any related features (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Name and profile picture (received from Google Sign-In)
  • Email address (received from Google Sign-In)
  • Messages you send in chat sessions (when you choose to save history)
  • Any personal information you voluntarily include in chat messages

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring/exit pages

1.3 Chat History (Optional)

Chat history is only saved when you are signed in. Conversations are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) under a key specific to your Google account ID. We do not upload or store your chat messages on our servers unless explicitly stated otherwise in a future update.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate you via Google Sign-In
  • To display your name and avatar in the interface
  • To save and restore your chat history (client-side only)
  • To communicate with you about the Service (rare)
  • To detect, prevent, or address technical issues or abuse

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with a valid legal request (court order, subpoena, etc.)
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of JackLinus AI, users, or the public
  • With service providers working on our behalf (analytics, hosting, etc.) under strict confidentiality

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Identity Services — used for sign-in. Subject to Google Privacy Policy.
  • Hugging Face — inference is powered by models hosted on Hugging Face Spaces. Subject to Hugging Face Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your chat history is stored only in your browser (localStorage) and never sent to our servers. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any data we do handle (logs, analytics metadata, etc.). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Delete History — You can delete individual chats or clear all history directly from the History menu while signed in.
  • Sign Out — Ends your session; local history remains until manually cleared.
  • Browser Data Clearing — Clearing localStorage / cookies removes all saved chat history.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" above.

Important: During the current launch phase, chat history is stored exclusively client-side (in your browser). Future updates may introduce server-side storage or additional features — any such change will be clearly communicated and will require your consent where required by law.
